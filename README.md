# Carbon Footprint Tracking System\n\n## Overview\n\nA comprehensive corporate carbon footprint tracking system built on the Stacks blockchain that enables companies to track, verify, and manage their carbon emissions data while facilitating carbon offset purchases and trading.\n\n## Features\n\n### Core Functionality\n- **Emissions Tracking**: Track corporate carbon emissions across all scopes\n- **Data Verification**: Blockchain-based verification of emissions data\n- **Carbon Offset Management**: Purchase and manage carbon offset credits\n- **Compliance Reporting**: Automated regulatory compliance reporting\n- **ESG Integration**: Environmental, Social, and Governance reporting\n- **Transparency**: Public transparency of corporate climate commitments\n\n### Smart Contract Features\n- **Carbon Tracking System**: Main contract for emissions and offset management\n- **Verified Data Storage**: Tamper-proof storage of emissions data\n- **Offset Credit Trading**: Marketplace for carbon offset credits\n- **Compliance Monitoring**: Automated compliance tracking and reporting\n- **Audit Trail**: Complete audit trail for all carbon-related transactions\n\n## Architecture\n\n### Contract Structure\n```\ncontracts/\n├── carbon-tracking-system.clar    # Main carbon tracking contract\n└── (Additional contracts as needed)\n```\n\n### Key Components\n1. **Emissions Recording**: Record and verify corporate emissions data\n2. **Offset Management**: Purchase, hold, and retire carbon offset credits\n3. **Compliance Tracking**: Monitor compliance with carbon regulations\n4. **Reporting System**: Generate automated compliance and ESG reports\n5. **Verification Network**: Third-party verification of emissions data\n\n## Getting Started\n\n### Prerequisites\n- Clarinet CLI installed\n- Node.js and npm\n- Basic understanding of Clarity smart contracts\n- Knowledge of carbon accounting principles\n\n### Installation\n1. Clone the repository\n2. Install dependencies: `npm install`\n3. Run tests: `clarinet test`\n4. Check contracts: `clarinet check`\n\n### Usage\n1. Deploy contracts to testnet/mainnet\n2. Register corporate entities for carbon tracking\n3. Record emissions data with verification\n4. Purchase carbon offsets for neutrality\n5. Generate compliance and sustainability reports\n\n## Contract Details\n\n### Carbon Tracking System Contract\nThe main contract handles:\n- Corporate emissions data recording and verification\n- Carbon offset credit management and trading\n- Compliance monitoring and automated reporting\n- Third-party verifier management\n- Carbon neutrality goal tracking\n- Audit trail maintenance\n\n### Security Features\n- Multi-signature verification for emissions data\n- Third-party auditor verification requirements\n- Immutable record keeping for compliance\n- Secure offset credit management\n- Prevention of double counting\n- Regulatory compliance automation\n\n## Testing\n\nRun the test suite:\n```bash\nclarinet test\n```\n\nCheck contract syntax:\n```bash\nclarinet check\n```\n\n## API Reference\n\n### Public Functions\n- `register-corporation`: Register a corporation for carbon tracking\n- `record-emissions`: Record verified emissions data\n- `purchase-offsets`: Purchase carbon offset credits\n- `retire-offsets`: Retire offsets for carbon neutrality\n- `verify-data`: Third-party verification of emissions data\n- `generate-report`: Generate compliance and sustainability reports\n- `set-carbon-goals`: Set corporate carbon reduction goals\n\n### Read-only Functions\n- `get-emissions-data`: Retrieve corporate emissions data\n- `get-offset-balance`: Check carbon offset holdings\n- `get-carbon-footprint`: Calculate total carbon footprint\n- `get-compliance-status`: Check regulatory compliance status\n- `get-verification-status`: Check data verification status\n- `get-sustainability-score`: Calculate sustainability performance score\n\n## Carbon Accounting\n\n### Scope Classifications\n- **Scope 1**: Direct emissions from owned/controlled sources\n- **Scope 2**: Indirect emissions from purchased energy\n- **Scope 3**: All other indirect emissions in value chain\n\n### Emission Categories\n- Energy consumption (electricity, heating, cooling)\n- Transportation (business travel, commuting, logistics)\n- Manufacturing processes and industrial activities\n- Waste generation and treatment\n- Supply chain and procurement\n\n### Verification Process\n1. **Data Collection**: Automated collection from corporate systems\n2. **Initial Verification**: Internal verification and validation\n3. **Third-Party Audit**: Independent verification by certified auditors\n4. **Blockchain Recording**: Immutable recording on blockchain\n5. **Public Reporting**: Transparent public disclosure\n\n## Offset Management\n\n### Offset Types Supported\n-
